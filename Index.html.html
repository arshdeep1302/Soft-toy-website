Meet the gang

Made with ♥ in Chandigarh

Crafted by artisans, loved by children everywhere

Snugglekins began in 2018 when founder Priya couldn't find a soft toy safe enough for her newborn. So she learned to stitch her own — and never stopped.

Every toy is sewn by hand by our small team of artisans in Chandigarh, using certified organic cotton and non-toxic, child-safe dyes. No shortcuts, no machines, no compromises.
